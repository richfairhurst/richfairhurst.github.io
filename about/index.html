About

Hiya! I’m Rich, I do cyber security stuff (for about 20 years at this point). Day to day that tends to include a range of activities around leadership and risk management although i’m most happy when knee deep in a debugger or decompiler. Sadly, that dones’t happen as much these days.

I’ve managed teams as small as 2 and larger than 100 across various different types of roles and always made it a case to never to lose my technical hands-on abilities. I’m a big fan of the two pizza box rule.

I enjoy taking complex problems and producing eloquent solutions, love a good CTF, and who’s not playing with AI at this point as well!
